FLY HIGH AIRLINES
About Us - Corporate Information
Fly High Airlines (India) is the aviation foray of the P & R Group. The airline operates its services under the brand FlyHigh. Fly High launched its operations in November 2009. Fly High, a low-fare carrier launched with the objective of commoditizing air travel. The airline currently operates across 10 destinations 40 daily flights and approximately 400 weekly flights.

The Fly High route network spans prominent business metropolises as well as key leisure destinations across the Indian subcontinent. Fly High is currently serving the airports at Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, Chandigarh, Delhi, Goa, Jaipur, Mumbai, Nagpur. Through this route network Fly High ensures its smart value-for-money option for both business and leisure travelers, without compromising on either safety or service factors.

Fly High has convenient online booking options wherein the passenger can book tickets 24x7, 365 days a year from the comforts of his home.

Fly High is positioned as 'the Smart People's Airline'. Its captivating theme, Fly Smart is aimed at offering passengers a consistent, quality-assured and time-efficient service through 'pocket-friendly' fares.

As part of its aggressive future outlook, Fly High plans to increase its fleet up to 20 aircrafts by the end of 2010 and to induct new routes while efficiently serving existing ones.
